Our firm has 12 years of experience in household organizing in preparation for palliative care, home hospice, or simply helping you and your loved ones to live a more minimalist lifestyle.

Our goals for your home:

  • Register the amount and type of items you have
  • Help you reach your downsizing and decluttering goals
  • Enable family and caregivers to better embrace a minimialist lifestyle for themselves, and their aging relative
  • Set manageable goals for removal of hoarded or no longer desirable objects while respecting the wishes of our client.
